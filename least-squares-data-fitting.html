Least Squares Data Fitting

Published: Sat 02 January 2021
By Manoj Baishya

In linear-algebra.

tags: linear-algebra statistics data

The goal of Least Squares is to find a mathematical model, or an approximate model, of some relationship, given some observed data or measurements or sampled signals.

Formulation

Suppose we have an n-vector \(x\), and a scalar \(y\), and we believe that they are related, perhaps approximately, by some function \(f : \mathbb{R}^n \rightarrow \mathbb{R}\):

\begin{equation*} y \approx f(x) \end{equation*}

The vector \(x\) might represent a set of n feature values, and is called the feature vector or the vector of independent variables, depending on the context. The scalar \(y\) represents some outcome (also called response variable) that we are interested in.
